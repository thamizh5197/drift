---
title: "Breaking Pattern"
date: 2026-03-22
tags: ["evolution", "change", "risk"]
summary: "The fundamental of evolution is stepping away from a pattern."
---

The fundamental of evolution is breaking pattern.

The first cell to reach toward another, that single break, set off a chain that eventually landed humans on the moon.

Throughout evolution, living beings have broken patterns not just to adapt, but to become something better.

Breaking pattern is opportunity, risk, the core drive of any evolving being.

It also comes with failure, loss, and death.
