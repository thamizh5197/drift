Fear being average

April 4, 2026 — philosophy, thinking

Do anything with passion.

Fear taking the path walked by thousands.

Do it all a step further than anyone else would.

Your only competition should be another passionate cunt going at it with everything he got.

Search, read, know, talk, teach. Never rest.

Be so thirsty for knowledge, art and philosophy or anything that the average doesn't entertain you enough.

Be so exposed and consumed that the average starves you.

Don't fool around unless it's a game. Do everything with passion.

Respect those not because of their background but on their passion for things.
