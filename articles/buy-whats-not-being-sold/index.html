Buy what's not being sold

March 26, 2026 — philosophy, thinking

You need love not marriage.
You need knowledge not education.
You need wealth not money.
You need happiness not peace.

Consumer markets sell you dreams that are not yours. They package what you actually want and give you a version of it that keeps you dependent.

You have to break the pattern first. Get lost. Define yourself. And only then build a life and a dream that is yours, not what is being sold.
